ESPHome Plant Monitoring Hardware

I build small ESP32 boards that report soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and light to Home Assistant using ESPHome. Highlights:

  • Capacitive soil moisture sensors with analog read averaging and calibration
  • SHT31 or BME280 for temp and humidity, optional BH1750 for light
  • Deep sleep for battery builds, high update rate for mains builds
  • MQTT state publishing, dashboards, and friendly notifications
  • OTA updates and 3D printed enclosures
